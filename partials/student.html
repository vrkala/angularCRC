We get it. Your job as a recruiter is to find talented candidates that will potentially turn into great hires for your organization. That's where Gator CareerLink comes in, it's your tool Let us introduce you to 50,000 University of Florida students.
Simply put, Gator CareerLink (or GCL as we affectionately call it) is the portal for everything career at UF. Look at GCL as your vehicle to get you in front of the thousands of students who want to connect with employers like yourself. Students use GCL to find job postings, career events, company information sessions and more, this is your opportunity to drive your message home to extremely qualified Gators.

Why Recruit at UF?

Recruiting at the University of Florida can provide you with access to a wide range of highly motivated and talented candidates. The Career Resource Center is the University of Florida's comprehensive centralized source to prepare and connect you to Hire UF Gators!
